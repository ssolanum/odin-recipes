Homemade fried chicken

This is a basic fried chicken recipe to enjoy alone or with friends

Ingredients

  • Chicken, cut into strips
  • eggs
  • flour
  • breadcrumbs
  • spices of choice
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Season the raw chicken with spices of choice
  2. Whisk the eggs and add the spices
  3. Dip the chicken into the flour, then the eggs, and into the breadcrumbs last
  4. Preheat your oil in a frying pan
  5. Fry your chicken until golden and crispy
