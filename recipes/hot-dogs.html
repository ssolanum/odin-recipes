Simple and quick hot dogs

Nothing like some good old basic hotdogs with ketchup and mustard

Altough not the fanciest, hotdogs are good and nostalgic, lets prepare some the good old fashioned way

Ingredients

  • Hotdogs of choice
  • Hotdog buns
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Boil or roast the hotdogs
  2. While they are boiling, toast the buns with some butter
  3. When the hotdogs are soft and slightly larger, drain them out
  4. Prepare the hotdog, squeeze ketchup and mustard on top
