Gnocchi

Buttery and soft homemade gnocchi

These gnocchi are pillowy and soft, the flavours of the butter and the herbs make a comfortable combination in your mouth and it's the perfect plate for a date at home

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Herbs of choice

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes and mash them
  2. Add the eggs and flour and mix until a dough forms
  4. Form the gnocchi by making long strips of dough and cutting litte pieces
  5. Boil the gnocchi until they float
  6. Toss them with butter and the herbs
